## Market Insights

### Brand Dominance
1. **CeraVe** - Most recommended by dermatologists (134-dermatologist panel winner)
2. **Cetaphil** - Second most recommended, strong in sensitive skin market
3. **La Roche-Posay** - Premium French pharmacy leader

### Key Trends (2026)
- **Barrier repair focus**: Ceramides, hyaluronic acid increasingly standard
- **Gentle surfactants**: SLS-free becoming the norm
- **pH-balanced**: Consumers more educated about acid mantle
- **Minimalist formulas**: Fragrance-free, fewer ingredients preferred
- **Drugstore dominance**: Expert consensus that luxury cleansers don't justify price premium

### Competitive Positioning
- **Budget leaders**: Neutrogena, Vanicream ($7-11)
- **Mid-market sweet spot**: CeraVe, Cetaphil ($10-15) - best value proposition
- **Premium justified**: La Roche-Posay ($15-18) - thermal water differentiation
- **Premium questionable**: Fresh, Dermalogica ($38-46) - sensory experience only
