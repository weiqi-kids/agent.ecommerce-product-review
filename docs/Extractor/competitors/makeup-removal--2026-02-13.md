## Competitive Positioning vs. Original Product (Neutrogena Wipes)

### Advantages Over Neutrogena Wipes:
1. **More effective cleansing** (especially oils and balms)
2. **Less irritating** (gentler on skin barrier)
3. **Better for environment** (reusable or glass packaging)
4. **Better value long-term** (balms/oils last 6+ months)
5. **No recall history** (unlike Neutrogena 2025 recall)

### Where Neutrogena Wipes Win:
1. **Convenience**: True on-the-go solution
2. **No water needed**: Useful for travel, camping
3. **Familiar**: Mainstream brand trust
4. **Cheap upfront cost**: $3-5 per pack

### Verdict:
Neutrogena wipes are only suitable for **emergency/travel use**. For daily makeup removal, cleansing balms, oils, or micellar water are **far superior** in effectiveness, skin health, and environmental impact.
