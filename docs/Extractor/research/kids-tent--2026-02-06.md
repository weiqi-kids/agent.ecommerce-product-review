1. **Developmental Significance:** Personal space crucial for identity development
2. **Multi-Year Use:** Good quality tents last through multiple childhood stages
3. **Versatile Benefits:** Imaginative play, sensory retreat, social scenarios
4. **Wide Price Range:** $20-$500+ requires guidance
5. **Safety Critical:** Enclosed spaces need careful safety consideration
6. **Space Investment:** Takes up significant room, purchase decision important

- Facilitates rather than directs play

- Long-term room impact

- Wide quality variance in market
