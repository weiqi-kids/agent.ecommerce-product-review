### Critical Safety Protocol

**MANDATORY STEPS BEFORE FIRST USE:**

1. **Verify Product Identity**
   - Confirm you received "Retinol Firming Serum Stick" (not lip product)
   - Check for included eye cream packette (mentioned in title)
   - Inspect roller ball tip for damage or crystal formation

2. **Patch Test (48-72 hours)**
   - Apply small amount to inner forearm
   - Wait 48 hours, monitor for redness/burning/rash
   - If any reaction occurs: DO NOT use on face
   - Even if patch test passes, start conservatively

3. **Initial Use Protocol**
   - Week 1: Apply every OTHER night only
   - Week 2: If no irritation, can increase to nightly
   - Target areas: Jawline, neck, chest, hands (avoid eye area unless using packette)
   - Follow with moisturizer and morning SPF 15+

4. **Ongoing Monitoring**
   - Inspect product before EACH use for crystal formation
   - If crystals appear: STOP use immediately (injury risk)
   - If burning sensation is severe or prolonged: discontinue
   - If redness/rash develops: stop use and consult dermatologist

5. **When to Stop Immediately**
   - Chemical burn sensation beyond mild tingle
   - Red streaks or spreading rash
   - Sharp particles/crystals in product
   - Severe dryness or skin cracking
   - Any discomfort beyond typical retinol adjustment
