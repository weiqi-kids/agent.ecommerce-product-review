### 問題詳細分析

#### 問題 #1: Clumping（結塊）— 34%

**問題描述**：睫毛膏在睫毛上形成結塊，造成「蜘蛛腳」或「tarantula leg」效果

**影響程度**：高 — 直接影響妝容外觀

**發生條件**：
- 刷頭沾取過多產品（最常見）
- 產品使用數次後開始變乾
- 多層塗抹時更明顯

**代表性引述**：
> "Incredibly clumpy and leaves my lashes looking like they are covered in thick black, chunky goop. Honestly, my eyelashes look much better with nothing on them."

> "It clumps them together because there's always too much mascara on the wand. The only way to solve the problem is to wipe off the excess before you apply it to your eyelashes, it works great after that, but you waste a lot of product."

> "The third time I used this product is started clumping on my eyelashes. I looked like a clown."

**可能原因**：
1. Flex Tower 刷頭設計過於柔軟，無法有效控制用量
2. 配方濃稠度過高
3. 產品品質控制不一致（批次差異）

**Workaround**：使用前在瓶口刮除多餘產品，或使用睫毛梳分離
