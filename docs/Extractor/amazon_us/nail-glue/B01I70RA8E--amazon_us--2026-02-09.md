## Overall Assessment

**Confidence Level**: Medium

This product exhibits a critical quality control issue with highly polarized user experiences. The 4.35 average rating masks extreme bimodality: users either love it (40% report 2-4 weeks longevity) or experience complete failure (36% report < 48 hours). The brush applicator design is genuinely innovative and well-received, but the inconsistent adhesion performance suggests:

1. **Possible batch quality variations** - Users buying multiple bottles report different performance
2. **High technique sensitivity** - Small differences in nail prep may drastically affect outcomes
3. **Product instability** - Bottle sealing issues indicate potential formula stability problems

The glue works exceptionally well for a significant portion of users, making it a "high risk, high reward" purchase. Success appears dependent on proper nail preparation (buffing, alcohol cleaning, avoiding water immediately after application) and potentially receiving a good batch.

**Recommendation**: Consider trying with low commitment given the inconsistency risk. Follow Nina Pool method and ensure thorough nail prep. Have backup glue available for events.
