### Issue Pattern 1: Ineffective for Cystic/Deep Acne
**Frequency**: 8/100 reviews (8%)
**Severity**: High (Core functionality failure for specific use case)

**Pattern Description**: Multiple users report complete ineffectiveness when used on cystic acne or deep, under-the-skin pimples.

**Evidence Quotes**:
- "Unfortunately, did nothing for my cystic acne even with a whitehead. It pulled out all the tiny hair on my cheeks and wasn't effective in any gunk removal." (R30CHAEVI9JMQG)
- "Deeper, under-the-skin pimples don't respond as well and often need more than one patch or a different treatment altogether." (R125YK9FB5JOYI)
- "Apply to almost exploding pimple. Removes all the pus. But not effective at new growing pimples." (RE48A10VFQ0G6)
- "As a physician...These work best on true whiteheads, not deep cystic acne." (R2AVG2OQA420FH)

**Root Cause**: Hydrocolloid patches require surface-level access to pimple contents. Deep acne lacks exposed whitehead for extraction mechanism to work.

**User Impact**: Users with cystic acne waste money on ineffective treatment. May delay seeking appropriate treatment for their specific acne type.

**Recommendation**: Product packaging/description should clearly state "for whiteheads and surface pimples only, not for cystic acne."
