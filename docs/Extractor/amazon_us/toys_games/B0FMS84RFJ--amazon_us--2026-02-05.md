### Product Description
"Where we're going, we don't need roads!" The LEGO Speed Champions Time Machine from Back to the Future (77256) building toy lets kids 9 years old and up create time travel adventures as they build, play with, or display 2 versions of the famous car from the blockbuster movie franchise. Young builders can construct the iconic DeLorean with its lightning rod and California license plate from the original 1985 adventure, then rebuild it into the flying version from the second movie, complete with Mr. Fusion, orange license plate, and those unforgettable sideways wheels. Both versions feature rear air vents, detailed cockpit, flux capacitor, and time calculator—everything needed to hit 88 miles per hour and travel through time! The buildable car toy includes Doc Brown and Marty McFly minifigures that fit inside the cockpit, and is packed with authentic details from both beloved films. Whether displayed as a collectible model or used for creative storytelling and pretend play adventures, this car model building kit makes an ideal gift for kids and fans. For even more fun, the LEGO Builder app provides digital guidance and 3D viewing tools. Contains 357 pieces.
