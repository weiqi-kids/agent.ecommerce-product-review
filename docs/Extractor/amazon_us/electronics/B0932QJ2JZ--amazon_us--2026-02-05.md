4. **battery_life** (88% positive, 8 mentions): Replaceable CR2032 batteries consistently last 12-18 months in real-world usage, meeting or exceeding Apple's "over a year" claim.

### Weaknesses

1. **anti_theft_effectiveness** (6% report): Apple's anti-stalking feature alerts nearby iPhone users when an unknown AirTag is traveling with them. 2. **tracking_delay_or_inaccuracy** (2% report): Some users experience delays in location updates when tracking items at a distance, particularly when the AirTag is stationary at home.

3. **defective_unit_outside_return_window** (2% report): One user received a defective unit in a 4-pack but discovered it after the 30-day return window, leaving them without recourse.

4. **deaf_accessibility_barrier** (2% report): The battery replacement reset process relies on audio cues, requiring deaf users to visit an Apple Store for assistance—creating a discriminatory barrier.

5. **battery_life_limited** (4% report): While most users are satisfied, some expected longer battery life or find the yearly replacement inconvenient.
