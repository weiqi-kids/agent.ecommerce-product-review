#### Recommended For:
- ✅ Fans of Harry Potter, Snoopy, or other licensed themes who value aesthetics
- ✅ Users seeking a soft, plush blanket that can optionally provide warmth
- ✅ People who want a blanket warm enough without heating (can use unplugged)
- ✅ Gift shoppers looking for themed presents for fans

#### Not Recommended For:
- ❌ Users who need intense heating (e.g., very cold climates, medical needs)
- ❌ People who require quick heat-up time (takes 10-20+ minutes)
- ❌ Those with beds where cord placement would be problematic
- ❌ Budget-conscious buyers ($88+ for potential heating issues)

#### Key Considerations Before Purchase:
1. **Heating expectations**: This blanket provides moderate warmth, not intense heat. If you need high-temperature heating, consider alternatives.
2. **Cord placement**: Check if top-corner plug location works with your bed/outlet setup. May need to flip blanket or use extension cord.
3. **Quality control**: ~10% defect rate for heating elements. Buy from retailer with good return policy.
4. **Price vs. performance**: You're paying premium for licensed design. Non-themed alternatives may offer better heating at lower cost.
