## L6: Synthesis & Strategic Insights

### Executive Summary

O'Keeffe's Working Hands Hand Cream demonstrates **exceptional market performance** for extreme dry hand care, with 80% five-star ratings and robust claim verification across effectiveness, texture, and scent dimensions. The product successfully occupies a **functional skincare niche** targeting manual laborers, healthcare workers, and cold-climate users who require intensive moisture repair without compromising work capability.

**Key Strengths**:
1. **Clinically-focused efficacy**: Users report healing of cracked hands within 2-3 days, outperforming "regular lotions"
2. **Professional-grade usability**: Non-greasy formula enables continued work with gloves, touchscreens, and tools
3. **Broad environmental resilience**: Effective across diverse use cases (cold weather, frequent handwashing, outdoor work, gym use)

**Risk Factors**:
