## L6: Evidence Summary

### Strengths
1. **Affordable Price Point** - Consistently praised as inexpensive and good value for basic framing needs, especially for temporary displays or children's rooms
2. **Easy to Use** - Simple to open, change photos, and hang; includes guitar pick tool for lifting tabs
3. **Appearance** - Clean, minimalist black design looks good on walls and blends with various decor styles
4. **Lightweight** - Easy to hang without strain on wall hooks
5. **Protective Film** - Plexiglass comes with protective film on both sides to prevent scratches during shipping
6. **Versatile Display** - Can accommodate 5x7 with mat or 8x10 without mat, horizontal or vertical mounting

### Weaknesses
1. **Poor Material Quality** - Made of cheap plastic or compressed wood pulp/dust, not quality MDF as claimed; corners stapled together
2. **Fragile Construction** - Very thin and flimsy; easily dented, scratched, or broken; multiple reports of frames breaking during use
3. **Low Build Quality** - Described as "dollar store quality" or worse by many users; not sturdy or durable
4. **Thin Plexiglass** - Paper-thin plastic that some users find unacceptable; sometimes has scratches or difficult-to-remove stickers
5. **Backing Mechanism Issues** - Metal tabs fall off or don't secure backing properly; users resort to tape
6. **High Damage Rate** - Significant number of units arrive broken, cracked, or damaged (10% in sample)
7. **Not Suitable for Canvas** - Too thin to accommodate canvas boards or thicker materials
8. **Moisture Sensitivity** - Swells when wet due to compressed wood construction
