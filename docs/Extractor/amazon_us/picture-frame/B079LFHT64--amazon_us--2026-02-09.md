## L5: Issue Patterns

### Critical Issues

#### 1. Incorrect Sizing (Frames Too Small)
- **Frequency**: 18/50 (36%)
- **Severity**: high
- **Pattern**: Frames consistently measure smaller than advertised size (4x6, 5x7, 8x10). Photos printed to standard dimensions require trimming or bending to fit. Issue affects multiple size variants.
- **User Impact**: Photos wrinkle, bubble, or require trimming. Cannot use professionally printed photos.
- **Affected Variants**: 4x6, 5x7, 8x10 frame sizes
- **Quality Control Concern**: One verified purchaser noted "I've bought these twice before and they have worked perfectly" but current batch is undersized, suggesting manufacturing inconsistency.

#### 2. Poor Build Quality / Frames Arrive Broken
- **Frequency**: 15/50 (30%)
- **Severity**: high
- **Pattern**: Frames arrive with corners separated, nails protruding, or completely fall apart when removed from packaging. Some break easily upon first drop.
- **User Impact**: Unusable product requiring returns or repairs.
- **Root Causes**:
  - Poor packaging/cushioning leading to shipping damage
  - Manufacturing defects (joints not properly secured)
  - Fragile construction

#### 3. Inadequate Packaging
- **Frequency**: 5/50 (10%)
- **Severity**: medium
- **Pattern**: Frames not properly cushioned between units, leading to shipping damage.
- **User Impact**: 2-3 frames per 5-pack arrive damaged.

### Medium Issues

#### 4. Wall Hanging Difficulty
- **Frequency**: 5/50 (10%)
- **Severity**: medium
- **Pattern**: Easel-back stand interferes with wall mounting. Stand difficult to remove and prone to breaking.
- **User Impact**: Frames won't hang flush against wall or straight.

#### 5. Frame Border Too Thick
- **Frequency**: 3/50 (6%)
- **Severity**: low
- **Pattern**: Some users find the frame border width detracts from photo visibility.
- **User Impact**: Aesthetic preference issue; reduces visible photo area.

#### 6. Incomplete Shipments
- **Frequency**: 1/50 (2%)
- **Severity**: high
- **Pattern**: Customer received only 1 frame instead of pack of 5.
- **User Impact**: Missing product requiring customer service intervention.
